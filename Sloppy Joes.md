# Sloppy Joes

## Ingredients

* 1 lb Ground Meat (Beef, Turkey, Lamb)
* Pinch Salt
* Pinch Pepper
* 1 tbs Flour
* 2/3 cup Ketchup
* 1 tbs Yellow Mustard
* 1 tsp Whole Grain Mustard
* 1 tbs Worcestershire Sauce
* 1 tsp Balsamic Glaze
* 1 tsp Apple Cider Vinegar
* 1 tsp Honey (Optional)
* 1 tsp Bourbon (Optional)
