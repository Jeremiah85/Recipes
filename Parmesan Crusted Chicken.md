# Parmesan Crusted Chicken

## Ingredients

* 2 lbs Chicken Breasts
* 1 tbs Olive Oil
* 1 Cup Panko Breadcrumbs
* 4 Chicken Breasts
* 1/3 Cup Mayonnaise
* 2 Large Eggs, Beaten
* 1/2 salt, plus for seasoning chicken
* 1/4 black pepper, plus for seasoning chicken
* 1 & 1/2 cups Grated Parmesan Cheese, Divided
* 1 tsp Garlic Powder
